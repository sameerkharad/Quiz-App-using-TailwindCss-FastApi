Quiz Rules

  • You will have 15 seconds to answer each question.
  • Once you select an option, you cannot change your answer.
  • If you fail to answer within the given time, the quiz will automatically move to the next question.
  • Each correct answer awards you 1 point.
  • Your final score will be displayed at the end of the quiz.
  • Enjoy the quiz and do your best!
